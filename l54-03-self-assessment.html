Your AI Readiness Self-Assessment: What Do You Know, What Are You Still Building?

Honest self-assessment is one of the most underrated professional skills. This assessment helps you see clearly where you're solid, where you're growing, and what to prioritize next — and gives you a personal learning plan you can take away.

Not everything in this certification will have landed at the same depth. Some concepts clicked immediately. Others are still coming into focus. Both of those are normal — and both are useful to know about yourself.

This assessment isn't a test with right and wrong answers. It's a structured self-reflection tool. Your honest ratings produce a personal learning plan — areas where you're ready to apply what you know right now, and areas worth revisiting or building further.

Honest self-assessment is itself a professional skill. Workers who can accurately describe what they know and what they're still learning are more trusted than those who overclaim. This assessment helps you practice that honesty with yourself first.

Rate Yourself Across Five Domains

For each competency, choose: Solid (I could explain this to someone and apply it on the job), Growing (I understand it but wouldn't feel fully confident using it yet), or Still Building (I need to revisit this — it hasn't clicked completely). Complete all items, then click "Generate My Plan."
